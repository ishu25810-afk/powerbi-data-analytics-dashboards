📌 Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a deep dive into Adidas sales performance across the United States. It tracks $900M in total sales revenue, analyzing the profitability, regional demand, and retailer performance from 2020 to 2021.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Sales: $900M
Operating Profit: $332M
Units Sold: 2M
Operating Margin: 42% (Average)

🔍 Visualizations & Insights
1. Sales Trends (Time Series)
Monthly Performance: Shows significant seasonality, with a major peak in July and a secondary surge in December, aligning with summer sports and holiday shopping.

2. Product & Retailer Performance
Top Product: Men's Street Footwear leads with $209M in sales, followed by Women's Apparel.

Top Retailer: West Gear is the dominant distributor ($0.24bn), followed closely by Foot Locker.

3. Regional & Geographic Distribution
Market Share: The West region accounts for the largest slice of sales at 30% ($270M).

Map View: Highlights high-density sales across the North American market, specifically focused on major metropolitan states.

🛠️ Technical Toolkit
Slicers: Region-based filtering and custom Date Range sliders.

Visuals: Area Charts (Trends), Donut Charts (Market Share), and Bar Charts (Rankings).
