🎬 Netflix Show Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
Designed to mirror the Prime Video analysis, this dashboard provides a comprehensive look at Netflix's massive content library. It focuses on the platform's shift toward original content and its dominance in the "Mature" (TV-MA) audience segment.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Titles: 8,802
Global Reach: Content from 749 Countries.
Creative Talent: 4,526 unique Directors.
Diversity: 515 Genres and 18 Rating types.

🔍 Visualizations & Insights
1. The Content Mix
Type Distribution: Netflix maintains a strong library of both Movies and TV shows, with a distinctive push into episodic series to drive user retention.
2. Content Maturity & Genres
Audience Targeting: TV-MA is the most frequent rating, showing Netflix's strategy to capture the adult demographic.
Top Genres: Dramas, International Movies, and Documentaries are the top three content categories by volume.
3. Global Production (Treemap)
Market Leaders: The United States (2.82K titles) and India (0.97K titles) are the primary content hubs, followed by the United Kingdom and Japan.
4. Historical Content Growth
The "Peak TV" Surge: The area chart reveals a massive explosion in content release volume between 2016 and 2020, specifically in the TV Show segment.

🚀 How to Use
Exploration: Use the Rating bar chart to filter the entire dashboard for specific age groups (e.g., Kids vs. Adults).

Regional Analysis: Click on a country in the Top 10 Countries treemap to see that specific nation's preferred genres and content types.
